Rental Benefits

  • Flexible rental terms (month-to-month or annual)
  • Maintenance and repair coverage included
  • Option to apply rental fees toward future purchase
  • Free size exchanges as students grow
  • Professional setup and tuning included

Rental Requirements

To rent an instrument from Strike a Chord:

  1. Valid photo ID (driver's license or passport)
  2. Credit card for security deposit
  3. Completed rental agreement form
  4. For students under 18: parent/guardian signature required
