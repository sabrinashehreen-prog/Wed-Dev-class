Our Music Instructors

At Strike a Chord, we take pride in our team of dedicated and professional music instructors. Each instructor is carefully selected for their expertise, teaching ability, and passion for music education.

Meet Our Team

Maria Garcia - Piano Instructor

With over 15 years of teaching experience, Maria specializes in classical piano and music theory. She holds a Master's degree in Music Education and has performed with several symphony orchestras.

James Wilson - Guitar Instructor

James has been teaching guitar for 12 years and specializes in rock, blues, and jazz styles. He has toured with multiple bands and brings real-world performance experience to his lessons.

Sarah Chen - Violin Instructor

Sarah is a classically trained violinist with 10 years of teaching experience. She teaches students of all ages and specializes in both classical and contemporary violin techniques.

David Miller - Voice & Theory Instructor

David holds a PhD in Music Theory and has been teaching voice and music theory for 8 years. He helps students develop proper technique and understand the fundamentals of music.

Our Teaching Philosophy

Our instructors believe in:

  • Creating a positive and encouraging learning environment
  • Tailoring lessons to each student's individual goals and learning style
  • Combining technical skill development with musical expression
  • Preparing students for performances and recitals
  • Making music education fun and rewarding

All our instructors are certified, background-checked, and participate in ongoing professional development to ensure they provide the highest quality music instruction.
